Controls

  • W: Use jetpack.
  • A, S: Move left/right
  • SPACE: Jump.
  • F: Shoot

How to play

You are trying to get to ÁTVR. But it's almost closing time. Try to get to the liquor store before 18:00. Dodge birds, rockets and cars that might be in your way. Collect beer bottles to gain powerups. Einstök gives you 3 additional bullets, Kaldi gives you a car and the gasoline bottle refills your jetpack fuel.
